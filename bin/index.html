OPTIONS INFORMATION:
Optimised Movement This is in accordance to 3.3 (LAB 2) requirements. Elevator will skip uppermost and/or lowermost floors should there be no further floors above or below to serve after searving a particular floor.
Active Scan The elevator will constantly scan all floors and immediately head towards the floor with an active button if it is not already on its way to serve a floor.
Energy Saving Elevator will pause at the nearest floor or the last served floor and await further active buttons before moving. 'Energy Saving' mode.
Drop Off Passengers are dropped off at their desired floor in addition to just being picked up. Floor to drop them off is decided randomly for now.


FLOOR BUTTON LEGEND:
RED Floor selected for pickup of passengers.
GREEN Floor has passengers that need to be dropped off at.
ORANGE Floor has BOTH passengers to pickup and dropoff.
